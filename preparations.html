Preparations for the cooking

Before we start we cooking, its important to be well prepared. Below are list of few things to note.

Cleanliness

Cleanliness is an important if you have a role in the culinary arts because it encourages food and kitchen safety, which have a benefits of helping you create a pleasant and healthy environment. As a cook, you can show your cleanliness by cleaning messes in your kitchen and sanitizing tools and dishes with the proper methods.

Food safety

Along with food and kitchen hygiene, ensuring proper food safety is paramount in a cooking position. This technical knowledge allows you to ensure food is safe for everyone to eat and prevent foodborne illnesses. For example, knowing what temperature to keep and cook foods safely can prevent spoiling or illness. You also usually uphold health codes, like keeping raw and cooked foods separate.

Adaptability

In a cooking, it's useful to be adaptable and ready to change your plans or ideas based on new information. Practice your adaptability skills by finding new ways to complete your work in different situations and learning to accept changes to your established methods.
