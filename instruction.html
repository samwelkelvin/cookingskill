Cooking Instructions

  • 1. Preheat your oven to 375Â°F.

  • 2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.

  • 3. Add the cold butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  • 4. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and stir until the dough comes together and forms a ball.

  • 5. Divide the dough in half and flatten each half into a disk.

  • 6. Wrap the disks in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  • 7. In a separate bowl, combine the sliced apples, sliced Asian pears, granulated sugar, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice. Toss to combine.

  • 8. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disk of dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch pie dish.

  • 9. Transfer the dough to the pie dish and trim the edges.

  • 10. Pour the apple and pear mixture into the pie crust.

  • 11. Dot the top of the filling with small pieces of butter.

  • 12. Roll out the second disk of dough and use a sharp knife or pastry cutter to cut it into strips.

  • 13. Arrange the strips over the filling in a lattice pattern.

  • 14. Brush the top of the pie with the beaten egg.

  • 15. Bake the pie for 50-60 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  • 16. Allow the pie to cool before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your delicious Asian Pear & Apple Pie!
